PRED_PREY_SIM is a collection of MATLAB files 

fd1d.m
fd1dKin2.m
fd1dx.m
fd1dxKin2.m
fd2d.m
fd2dKin2.m
fd2dx.m
fd2dxKin2.m

for solving some reaction-diffusion systems modelling predator-prey
interactions in 1D and 2D, using finite element / difference
methods. PRED_PREY_SIM is Copyright (C) 2005 Marcus R.Garvie.
